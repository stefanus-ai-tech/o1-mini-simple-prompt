Roadmap

Step-by-step guide to becoming a successful penetration tester.

Step 1: Education

Obtain a degree or certifications in cybersecurity or a related field.

Step 2: Learn Essential Tools

Familiarize yourself with tools like Nmap, Metasploit, Burp Suite, and Wireshark.

Step 3: Gain Practical Experience

Participate in labs, Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, and real-world projects.

Step 4: Specialize

Choose a specialization such as web application pentesting, network pentesting, or mobile security.

Step 5: Professional Development

Obtain advanced certifications like OSCP, CEH, or CISSP and seek employment in the field.

Step 1: Education

Education is the first crucial step in your pentest roadmap. You can pursue a degree in Computer Science, Information Security, or related fields. Additionally, obtaining certifications like CompTIA Security+, Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH), or Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) can significantly enhance your knowledge and credibility.

  • Degree Programs (BSc, MSc in Cyber Security)
  • Certifications (CEH, OSCP, CISSP)
  • Online Courses and Workshops
